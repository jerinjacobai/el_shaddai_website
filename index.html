Welcome to El-Shaddai

A Place of Faith, Hope, Worship & Transformation

We are delighted that you are here. Whether you are seeking spiritual guidance, looking for a church family, exploring the Christian faith, or simply desiring to grow closer to God, we warmly welcome you.

El-Shaddai Ministries is a Christ-centered ministry founded through God's divine vision in 2016. Originally established as Indo Nepal Church of God, God faithfully expanded the ministry, bringing people from different nations, languages, and cultures into our fellowship.

The name "El-Shaddai" means "God Almighty" — the all-sufficient God who provides, protects, and empowers His people. Today, we are committed to proclaiming the Gospel, making disciples, and serving communities across cultural and linguistic boundaries.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

El-Shaddai Ministries envisions a world transformed by the Gospel of Jesus Christ, where individuals, families, and communities experience spiritual renewal, biblical truth, and the abundant life found in God.

Our Mission

El-Shaddai Ministries exists to glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, making disciples, nurturing believers in faith and spiritual maturity, fostering Christ-centered fellowship, serving communities with compassion, and empowering people through the power of the Holy Spirit to fulfill God's purpose and advance His Kingdom.

Our Motto

"Proclaiming Christ, Transforming Lives, Advancing God's Kingdom." — We are committed to building a ministry where worship is authentic, prayer is powerful, the Word of God is faithfully preached, and every believer is empowered to fulfill God's divine purpose.

Our Reach

We welcome people from all nations, backgrounds, and walks of life. Our vision is rooted in the Great Commission — to make disciples of all nations, teaching them to obey all that Christ has commanded. Serving God. Serving People. Impacting Nations.

What We Believe

Statement of Faith

"For no one can lay any foundation other than the one already laid, which is Jesus Christ." — 1 Corinthians 3:11

01

The Scriptures Inspired

The Bible, consisting of sixty-six books, is the inspired Word of God, the infallible rule of faith and conduct. (2 Tim. 3:15-17)

02

The One True God

The one true God has revealed Himself as the eternal, self-existent "I AM," embodying the Trinity as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. (Matt. 28:19)

03

Jesus Christ

Born of a virgin, His sacrificial death on the cross provided atonement for sin. He was resurrected and ascended to intercede as our High Priest. (Gal. 4:4-5)

04

The Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit is the Lord and Giver of life, who proceeds from the Father and the Son, and together is worshiped and glorified. (John 14:16-17)

05

Salvation of Mankind

Those who repent and accept Jesus Christ as Savior are justified by grace through faith, becoming children of God and co-heirs with Christ. (Rom. 10:13-15)

06

Baptism in Water

Baptism by immersion in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit is observed by all who have repented and accepted Christ. (Matt. 28:19)

07

The Baptism of the Holy Spirit

All believers should earnestly seek the Baptism of the Holy Spirit with the endowment of power for life and service. (Acts 1:8)

08

Divine Healing

Deliverance from sickness is provided in the atonement and is the privilege of all believers. (Isaiah 53:4; James 5:14-16)

09

The Blessed Hope

The pre-millennial second coming of Jesus Christ and the rapture of the Church is the blessed hope of all believers. (1 Thess. 4:16-18)

10

The Church

The Church is the body of Christ, the habitation of God through the Holy Spirit, appointed for the fulfillment of the Great Commission. (Eph. 1:22-23)
